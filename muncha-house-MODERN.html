Our Story

A Century of
Family Business


Muncha House was founded in 1925 with a clear purpose — to provide quality everyday products at fair prices, backed by honest relationships. What began as a small neighbourhood trading post has grown, generation by generation, into a trusted name in retail and wholesale supply.

We source carefully, stock reliably, and serve personally. Whether you're an individual customer or a business placing bulk orders, you'll find the same four generations of care and commitment behind every transaction.

📦

Retail & wholesale under one roof. Flexible for individuals and business buyers alike — with the personal touch only a century-old family business can offer.
